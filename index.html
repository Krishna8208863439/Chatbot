    Tomato Early Blight

    Host Crop: Tomato (Solanum lycopersicum)

    94% Match
    Observed Symptoms

    Dark circular spots with concentric rings ('target board' appearance) starting on older lower leaves. Surrounding tissue is chlorotic (yellowing).

    Organic/Biological Treatment

    Spray liquid copper fungicide or Bacillus subtilis formulations early morning. Prune the lower infected leaves to improve air circulation.

    Eco-Friendly Bacillus formulation
    Chemical Treatment (Emergency)

    Apply Chlorothalonil or Mancozeb fungicide at 2g per liter water. Repeat spray after 10 days if rain persists.

    Class II Fungicide Chlorothalonil
    Prevention Strategy
    • Implement 3-year crop rotation (avoid nightshade family).
    • Use drip irrigation to prevent leaves from remaining wet.
    • Apply balanced potassium fertilizers to increase resistance.
